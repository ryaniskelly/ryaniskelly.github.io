About Me:


Born and raised in South Florida. Moved to Boston because I was sick of sunshine.
Attended BU Law, where I learned from day one to forecast every conceivable thing
that can go wrong for a client, then protect against it. Yeah, attorneys tend to be a
pessimistic group, and I was never comfortable with that notion. What attracted me
to startups is the need to not only be optimistic and embrace risk, but to believe that
the impossible can be done.
